01. About Me

I'm a Senior Product Manager at HashiCorp focused on the intersection of Generative AI and infrastructure automation. I lead product strategy for AI-powered developer experiences, including the Terraform MCP Server that enables AI agents to manage infrastructure.

My expertise spans agentic AI systems, MCP (Model Context Protocol) servers, and building intelligent automation that augments developer workflows. I'm passionate about creating tools that leverage LLMs and AI agents to solve complex infrastructure challenges.
